Preparing a Sandwhich

  1. Set aside two pieces of your preferred type of bread.
  2. Find your favorite ingredients to include in the sandwhich. Examples include:
    • Meat
    • Cheese
    • Lettuce
    • Tomato
    • Peanut Butter
    • Bananas
  3. Place your selected ingredients atop one of the slices of bread
  4. Put the other slice of bread on top of everything else
  5. Enjoy!
